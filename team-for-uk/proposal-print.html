Special Requirements & Dependencies

Android 15 (API Level 35) Compliance

Google Play Store requires all new app submissions to target Android 15. SDK upgrade, dependency audit, and Play Store re-submission required. Addressed across Sprint 2–3 by the mobile engineer.

Microsoft SSO — Global Integration

Requires OAuth2 app registration in Azure, callback URL configuration, org-claim mapping, and audit logging. Scheduled in Sprint 5.

UK GDPR — Account Deletion & Data Erasure

Article 17 (Right to Erasure) — user deletion requests from both in-app and Intercom channels must be fully processed. Currently broken for both vectors. Sprint 1 priority.

Intercom, Sentry & Codebase Access

To work at full velocity from day one, we require: Git repository access, staging environment credentials, Sentry project access, and Intercom workspace access. Onboarding call with the current technical team is expected in Week 1.

Governing Body Standards Versioning

GMC, HCPC, AHPRA, CMI, BACP, and others maintain their own CPD standards formats which change periodically. Standards changes require coordinated content and data migration — not just a front-end text swap.

Risk Mitigation — What Happens If the Timeline Slips

Sprint Overrun — Task Carries Over, Not Dropped

Unfinished tasks roll into the next sprint as a priority item. Leni reports the carryover in the weekly progress report with a revised estimate and root cause.

Scope Change — Backlog Reprioritisation, Not Silent Additions

If new tasks are added mid-sprint, we flag the trade-off: something of equal weight moves to the next sprint, or we extend with CPDme's agreement.

External Dependency Blocker — Escalated Within 24 Hours

If progress is blocked by a third-party outage, missing credentials, or a decision needed from CPDme — we flag it in standup the same day and escalate via Slack/Teams if unresolved within 24 hours.

Critical Bug in Production — Dropped Into Current Sprint Immediately

Any Sentry-flagged production error gets pulled into the active sprint immediately, displacing a lower-priority backlog item. Production stability takes precedence over scheduled features.
