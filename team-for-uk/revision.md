1. Tambahkan Executive Summary (di halaman paling atas)

Letakkan sebelum semua section.

Contoh:

We are a dedicated SaaS engineering team providing a full-time remote development team to support CPDme.

Our focus is to reduce backlog, improve platform stability, and accelerate feature delivery — while ensuring compliance with UK GDPR and maintaining high system reliability.

This proposal outlines a structured 8-month delivery plan, aligned with your current backlog and business priorities, delivered within the £10,000/month budget.

⸻

2. Tambahkan Why Our Team (Differentiation)

Letakkan setelah “What’s Included” atau sebelum “Team Overview”.

Contoh:

Why Our Team

* We analysed your real backlog and translated it into a structured delivery plan (not a generic proposal)
* We provide a fully dedicated team aligned with UK working hours
* We focus on ownership and long-term stability, not just feature delivery
* We operate as an embedded team, collaborating closely with your stakeholders
* We prioritise production stability and fast response to critical issues

⸻

3. Tambahkan Relevant Experience (Proof)

Tidak perlu panjang, cukup 4–6 bullet.

Contoh:

Relevant Experience

* Delivered and maintained SaaS platforms with subscription-based models
* Built and supported systems involving authentication (SSO, OAuth2) and secure data handling
* Experience handling production systems with real users and ongoing feature delivery
* Delivered mobile and web applications with continuous deployment workflows
* Experience working in remote teams with international stakeholders

⸻

4. Tambahkan Commercial Model (Clarity Budget)

Letakkan sebelum closing.

Contoh:

Commercial Model

This proposal is designed to align with your stated budget of £10,000/month.

It includes a dedicated full-time team (as outlined above), covering all development, QA, DevOps, and project coordination activities.

No additional resource cost is required for standard delivery. Any scope changes will be discussed and agreed transparently.

⸻

5. Softening Timeline (hindari terlihat terlalu optimis)

Tambahkan 1 kalimat di section timeline kamu:

Tambahan kalimat:

This timeline is flexible and will be adjusted based on real-world system complexity, evolving priorities, and findings during onboarding.

⸻

6. Tambahkan sedikit Business Language (di beberapa bagian)

Ubah beberapa kalimat teknis jadi ada impact bisnis.

Contoh:

* ❌ “bug fixing and feature development”
* ✅ “improving platform stability and accelerating feature delivery”
* ❌ “implement SSO”
* ✅ “ensure secure and scalable authentication across organisations”

⸻

7. Perbaiki Closing (biar lebih “confident”)

Ganti bagian akhir kamu jadi:

We are ready to act as a long-term engineering partner for CPDme, supporting both your current backlog and future growth.

We believe our structured approach, dedicated team, and strong ownership mindset make us a reliable and low-risk partner.

We look forward to discussing how we can start working together.
